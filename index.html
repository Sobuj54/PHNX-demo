Frequently Asked.

Does repair void my warranty? +
We use OEM-spec parts and provide a lifetime guarantee on our hardware. Most modern regulations protect your right to repair without voiding device integrity.
How long does a logic board repair take? +
Complex micro-soldering typically takes 24-48 hours depending on the extent of IC damage and trace restoration required.
Can you recover data from water damage? +
Yes. We specialize in ultrasonic cleaning and NAND transplantation to recover data from catastrophic liquid ingress.
